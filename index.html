Camera Recording Portal

Silent camera recording uploaded directly from this browser to Google Drive. The Drive link is then written directly to Google Sheets.

● RECORDING — VIDEO ONLY 00:00 / 00:20
Add the Google OAuth Client ID in this project's index.html, deploy to Cloudflare Pages, then sign in with an account that can edit the target Drive folder and Google Sheet.

Maximum recording duration: 20 seconds. Microphone is disabled. Recommended target size: under 3 MB per recording.
